Meet Our Leaders

The Visionaries Behind Our Success

Our leadership team combines decades of industry expertise with a passion for innovation and excellence in construction.

Mr. Amit Anand

Managing Director

Leadership Profile

Mr. Amit Anand is the managing director and founder and has always been the guiding force behind the company. A management graduate, he brings discipline and a strong operational focus to every project. Since the company's early days, he has followed a steady, balanced growth path. In 2010 he began his career by undertaking civil works for municipal corporations and PWD, and later accumulated further experience by executing projects such as upgrading railway lines in Bihar and other infrastructure works.

Mr. Anand earned credibility by working with several reputed national and local construction firms on prestigious assignments including highways and ROB (railway over bridge) projects. In 2014 he partnered with PunjLoyd for work on National Highway-28, and in 2016 he founded his own private company, leveraging his accumulated experience and industry insight to deliver reliable infrastructure solutions.

Key Achievements: The company's beginning was modest, but over time it has grown stronger to become one of the fastest-growing firms in the sector. We focus on creating clear value for clients and adhere to strict discipline to ensure timely completion. Notably, we executed 2 lakh cubic meters of earthwork excavation in hard rock within 120 days and have completed packages on the Lucknow–Agra Expressway in collaboration with L&T.

Mr. Sushant Kumar

Executive Director

Leadership Profile

Mr. Sushant Kumar, our Executive Director, is an engineering and management graduate with varied experience across multiple infrastructure projects. He is responsible for creating a broad technical roadmap for our civil and mechanical projects and plays a crucial role in managing various operational aspects of the organization, including designing and developing business plans in close collaboration with the board.

Mr. Kumar has a significant role in managing internal committees and works closely with his staff to streamline implementation frameworks and ensure project delivery standards are met.

Key Responsibilities: Technical roadmap creation, operational management, committee oversight, and close coordination with the board for business planning and implementation.

Anutham Buildcon Pvt Ltd is a construction company established on 28th August, 2019 for the purpose of carrying out various activities related to construction of Roads and Bridges. It is a unit of Once More Construction Pvt Ltd.
